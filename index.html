Why Families Love Bernedoodles

Bernedoodles combine the best of Bernese Mountain Dogs and Poodles — loyal, gentle, and strikingly beautiful.

👶

Gentle Giants With a Soft Side

Bernedoodles inherit the calm, affectionate nature of the Bernese Mountain Dog. They are gentle with children, patient with other pets, and deeply loyal to their families.

✨

Low-Shedding Coats

Thanks to their Poodle heritage, Bernedoodles tend to shed much less than other large breeds. Curlier coats shed the least, though no dog is truly hypoallergenic.

🎓

Smart & Eager to Please

Bernedoodles are highly intelligent and love making their owners happy. They pick up training quickly and respond well to positive reinforcement.

❤️

Playful Yet Calm

Bernedoodles have the perfect balance of energy and calmness. They love playtime and outdoor adventures but are equally happy curling up on the couch with you.
